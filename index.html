Who am I?

My name is Dante Proto. I was born December 6th, 1994 in Mesa, AZ. I currently live in Queen Creek, AZ, where I grew up. I attended St. Timothy's Catholic Academy for elementary school. I went to a public school for the first time in the 7th grade at Payne Jr. High. It was around this time when I started playing my all time favorite sport, American Football. After football season, I started practicing in the art of Roman Greco wrestling. My Jr. High sports career prepared me for High School level competition. I had a very successful Freshman football season. I moved up to the Junior Varsity level, which ended up being where my football career ended. There is not a day that goes by that I do not miss playing! I did wrestle all 4 years of high school, and had a phenomenal varsity season! I barely missed my chance at going to the state wrestling tournament(by 3 points) in the sectional finals. Sports taught me a lot about discipline, work ethic, and how to be a part of a team. I also showed livestock starting at 9 years old until I was 18 years old. One of my greatest accomplishments was winning Reserve Grand Champion for my market hog.

After highschool, I wanted to be a mechanical engineer due to inspiration from my neighbor. I quickly realized that calculus was a big part of engineering, and it was my least favorite class in college. I switced my major to General Business after my first year of college. I have been working towards becoming an accountant for some years now. I have not obtained a degree in business yet. However, I did study part time for 3 years while working fast food at Panda Express, and later working retail at Costco. I worked in Front End operations, and I was a sales representative in the electronics department before I left Costco to drive Uber and Lyft full time. It was fun for awhile, however, the hours are hard to work, and the wear and tear on your vehicle really adds up over time. I do have some wonderful stories though! I landed a job with Dish Network as a Customer Service representative. I handled customer service calls until I was later promoted to technical support. I then handled a mix of tech and customer service calls. I was excited when chat became an opportunity at Dish. I was a part of the first 2 chat teams that were created at the Phoenix location. I handled a mix of tech and customer service chats. I enjoy chat support a lot more than phone calls, as it seems to be more efficient, and I can be more descriptive to help the customer to the best of my ability. Chat has since closed at our location, and I am now a Fulfillment agent. I schedule technicians and order parts for Samsung Appliance repair, as we are an authorized service provider.

I am now looking to advance into a better paying career, that not only pays the bills, but that I enjoy! I have been attending UA Coding Bootcamp, to obtain my certificate in Full Stack Web Development. I am through the Front End portion of my course, and I am half way through the entire course. I have experience with HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, Firebase DB, SQL and My SQL DB, Node.js, and using API's from multiple sources. I love writing code, and have worked hard on this page to display my new skills. I have used Bootstrap, and my own CSS on this page. I have added some animations using an awesome website called Animista. I plan to continue adding more to this page, and would like to write some functionality for it to better display my skills.
